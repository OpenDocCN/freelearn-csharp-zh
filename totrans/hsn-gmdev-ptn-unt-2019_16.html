Section 5: Structural Patterns

In this section, we will explore Structural patterns, which will permit to us to adapt, extend, and connect game systems together that were built by various programmers so that we can reuse them in multiple projects. The design patterns in this book will teach you skills that will permit you to work with legacy code.
