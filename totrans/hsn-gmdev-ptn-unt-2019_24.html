Section 7: Optimization Patterns

It's by mastering optimization that programmers in the gaming industry prove themselves and gain mastery. Unity might come pre-optimized on certain aspects, but it can be a trap because performance issues are often subtle and only appear after deploying on your target platform. So, getting into the habit of implementing the right design patterns early on in the production process might save you from having to crunch for long hours just before launch day.
